Microwave Convection Cooking

  • --Use Round Metal Grill Rack on turntable--
    (This elevates food for even browning) or Use baking Rack (slides in 1/2 way up.)
  • Put food in container allowing airflow.
  • 1. Touch EASY CONVECT.
  • 2. Choose food by numer, see chart.
  • 3. Enter Temperture, then time with numbers.
  • 4. Press START. (NO FOOD - Preheating begins.)
  • (When preheating ends, display says INSERT FOOD.)
    5. Insert food, touch START.
